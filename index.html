REQUIREMENTS FOR BUILDING PERMIT:

I. Any person desiring to obtain a buidling permit shall file an application therefore in writing and on the prescribed form.

A. Together with the accomplished application form the following shall be submitted to the Office of the Building Official:

  1. Certified true copy of TCT
  2. Tax Declaration
  3. Current Real Property Tax Receipt

B. In case the applicant is not the registered owner of the lot:

  1. Duly notaeized copy of the Contract of Lease
  2. Duly notarized copy of the Deed of Absolute Sale
  3. Duly notarized copy of the contract of Sale

2. FIVE (5) sets of plans and specification prepared, signed and sealed

  1. by a duly licensed Architect, in case of architectural plans
  2. by a duly licensed Civil or Structural Engineer in case of structural plans
  3. by a duly licensed Sanitary Engineer or Master Plumber, in case of plumbing or sanitary installation plans
  4. by a duly licensed professional Electrical Engineer, in case of electrical plans
  5. by a duly licensed professional Mechanical Engineer, in case of mechanical plans
  6. bill of materials/ Construction Safety and Health Program & specifications duly signed and Architect /Civil Engineer

For any of the above, a simple sketch with dimensions, accompanied by a site location and vicinity map, will suffice.
